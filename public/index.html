What is LinkMyProf

The project LinkMyProf aims at helping people look for all kinds of information about professors: their research interests, their emails, their publications, what kind of PHD students they are looking for currently, etc.

We believe that this kind of assistance is crucial when a student starts to find a interesting research topic or have a problem to decide which university they should attend. They can search their matches in our data base and email the professors directly. Professors, on the other hand, can post information on their personal webpage and deliver it to all student interested in his project.

It is the combination of LinkedIn and Rate My Professor.

How do we work

To get the data we need, we will firstly write a script to extract professor’s information from the websites.

We need professor’s name, university address, department and email address and their research interest at least. Also, we can put their personal website in our database. We will get all of these data from universities’ official websites where they post faculty and staff information.

As for the advanced function, we currently have the following ideas:

Firstly, I believe our information extraction algorithm will become our first advanced function. Since university’s websites usually have different structures, we need to write script dealing with these different situations.

Secondly, we are considering auto match students and professor’s research. It should not sole rely on key word search, but data mining algorithms that use Cosine distance as the base, which are still to be determined.
